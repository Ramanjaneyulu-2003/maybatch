Episode Description

Vice President and Head of ETFs, Alan Green is joined by Executive Director, The Canadian ETF Association, and Head of Dynamic Funds, Mark Brisley, to discuss the recent industry ETF Exchange Conferences and the key emerging themes. They discuss the evolving landscape of ETFs, including the growing momentum of actively managed ETFs and the shifting focus from passive to active strategies. With the ETF industry surpassing $1D trillion in assets, these industry leaders provide insights on the future of ETFs and the opportunities ahead.
